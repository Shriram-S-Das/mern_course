London

London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is a 21st-century city with history stretching back to Roman times. At its centre stand the imposing Houses of Parliament, the iconic ‘Big Ben’ clock tower and Westminster Abbey, site of British monarch coronations.

Rome

It's located in the Lazio region, on the Tiber River, and is renowned for its ancient ruins, including the Colosseum and Roman Forum, as well as its artistic and architectural achievements. Rome is also home to Vatican City, the sovereign state and headquarters of the Catholic Church
